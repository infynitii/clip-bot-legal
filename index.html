Marqxi Clip Bot

Private clip-to-TikTok publishing for a single operator.

Marqxi Clip Bot is a private, personal automation tool. It helps its single operator turn their own longer videos and livestream recordings into short clips, and — once the operator has reviewed and approved a clip — publishes it to the operator's own TikTok account through the official TikTok Content Posting API. It is not a consumer product and has no users other than the operator.

What it does

  • Generates short vertical/widescreen clips from the operator's own source video.
  • Presents each clip to the operator in a private review dashboard.
  • Publishes only clips the operator explicitly approves — nothing is posted automatically without that approval.
  • Posts solely to the operator's own connected TikTok account.

How it uses TikTok

  • Login Kit — the operator connects their own TikTok account via TikTok's OAuth flow.
  • Content Posting API (Direct Post) — approved clips are uploaded and published with the video.publish scope.
  • The only scopes requested are user.info.basic and video.publish. The App never reads followers, messages, viewing history, or any other user's data.

Data & privacy

The App accesses only what it needs to post the operator's approved clips to the operator's own account. OAuth tokens are stored privately on the operator's own server, are never shared with third parties, and are deleted on request or when access is revoked. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

Privacy Policy

Marqxi Clip Bot · Effective 2 June 2026

Marqxi Clip Bot ("the App", "we", "us") is a private, personal automation tool that publishes short video clips to the operator's own TikTok account using the TikTok Content Posting API. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Scope

The App is operated by a single individual for the sole purpose of posting the operator's own content to the operator's own TikTok account. It is not a consumer product, has no end users other than the operator, and does not collect data from any other person.

Information We Access

  • TikTok account authorization. When you authorize the App, TikTok provides an OAuth access token and refresh token scoped to user.info.basic, video.upload, and video.publish.
  • Basic profile information. Limited to what user.info.basic returns (e.g. account display name and the privacy options available to the account), used only to determine valid posting settings.
  • The videos you choose to publish. Clips you explicitly approve are uploaded to your TikTok account through the Content Posting API.

We do not access your followers, your messages, your viewing history, or data about any other TikTok user.

How We Use It

Access is used exclusively to upload and publish clips that the operator has approved, to the operator's own account. We do not use the data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to posting.

Storage and Retention

OAuth tokens are stored privately on the operator's own server and are used only to make authorized API calls. They are never transmitted to any third party. Tokens are retained only as long as the App is in use and are deleted when access is revoked or on request.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any accessed data with third parties. The only external service the App communicates with is TikTok's own API.

Revoking Access and Deleting Data

You may revoke the App's access at any time from TikTok under Settings → Security & permissions → Apps and websites. To request deletion of any stored tokens or data, contact us at the address below; stored credentials are deleted promptly on request.

Security

Credentials are kept on a private, access-controlled server and are not exposed to the public internet. Reasonable measures are taken to protect them from unauthorized access.

Children

The App is not directed to and is not used by anyone under the age of 18.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
